# Marketing Plan for Estate-Mapper
## Positioning
### Primary Positioning Statement
"Estate-Mapper: Automate legacy IT asset discovery, ownership attribution, and decommission-safety with AI-driven insights."

### Alternative Positioning Statements
1. "Unlock IT efficiency with Estate-Mapper's AI-powered estate discovery and decommissioning solution."
2. "Estate-Mapper: Simplify legacy IT asset management with real-time usage and ownership insights."
3. "Transform your IT operations with Estate-Mapper's AI-driven estate mapping and risk assessment capabilities."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: IT Operations Manager - Rachel Chen
* Daily Job: Overseeing IT infrastructure and operations for a mid-sized enterprise.
* Budget: $100,000 - $500,000 per year for IT asset management and optimization tools.
* Pain Points: Manual IT asset discovery, ownership disputes, and decommissioning risks.

### Persona 2: Cloud Security Architect - David Kim
* Daily Job: Designing and implementing cloud security architectures for large enterprises.
* Budget: $500,000 - $2,000,000 per year for cloud security and compliance tools.
* Pain Points: Ensuring legacy IT assets comply with cloud security standards and regulations.

### Persona 3: IT Asset Manager - Emily Patel
* Daily Job: Managing IT asset inventory and lifecycle for a large enterprise.
* Budget: $200,000 - $1,000,000 per year for IT asset management and optimization tools.
* Pain Points: Accurately tracking IT asset ownership and decommissioning risks.

## Channels
### Primary Channels
1. **Content Marketing**: Estimated CAC: $50 - $100 per lead.
	* Blog posts (2 per week): "Estate-Mapper: A Game-Changer for Legacy IT Asset Management" and "How AI-Driven Insights Simplify IT Operations."
	* Guest posts on industry blogs (2 per month): "The Importance of IT Asset Discovery in Cloud Security" and "AI-Powered IT Asset Management: A New Era."
2. **Social Media**: Estimated CAC: $20 - $50 per lead.
	* Twitter: Share Estate-Mapper updates, industry news, and thought leadership content (5 tweets per week).
	* LinkedIn: Share Estate-Mapper case studies, industry insights, and thought leadership content (3 posts per week).
3. **Email Marketing**: Estimated CAC: $10 - $20 per lead.
	* Newsletter (bi-weekly): Share Estate-Mapper updates, industry news, and thought leadership content.

## Content Cadence
### Week 1-4: Awareness and Interest
* Blog posts: "Estate-Mapper: A Game-Changer for Legacy IT Asset Management" and "How AI-Driven Insights Simplify IT Operations."
* Social media: Share Estate-Mapper updates, industry news, and thought leadership content.
* Email marketing: Send a welcome email with a link to the Estate-Mapper website.

### Week 5-8: Consideration and Evaluation
* Guest posts on industry blogs: "The Importance of IT Asset Discovery in Cloud Security" and "AI-Powered IT Asset Management: A New Era."
* Social media: Share Estate-Mapper case studies, industry insights, and thought leadership content.
* Email marketing: Send a series of educational emails highlighting Estate-Mapper's features and benefits.

### Week 9-12: Purchase and Onboarding
* Blog posts: "Estate-Mapper: A Real-World Example of AI-Driven IT Asset Management" and "How to Get Started with Estate-Mapper."
* Social media: Share customer testimonials, success stories, and thought leadership content.
* Email marketing: Send a series of onboarding emails with tips and resources for getting started with Estate-Mapper.

## Launch Milestones
### D-30 (30 Days Before Launch)
* Finalize marketing materials, including the website, social media assets, and email templates.
* Establish a content calendar and schedule content for the next 90 days.

### D-0 (Launch Day)
* Launch the Estate-Mapper website and social media channels.
* Send a launch email to the email list with a link to the website and a special promotion.

### D+30 (30 Days After Launch)
* Analyze launch metrics, including website traffic, social media engagement, and email open rates.
* Adjust the marketing strategy based on the analysis.

### D+90 (90 Days After Launch)
* Evaluate the success of the marketing strategy, including metrics such as DAU and $MRR.
* Plan for future marketing initiatives and content creation.

## Success Metrics
### DAU (Daily Active Users)
* Target: 100 DAU within the first 90 days.

### $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
* Target: $10,000 MRR within the first 90 days.

Note: These targets are based on conservative estimates and may need to be adjusted based on the actual performance of the marketing strategy.
